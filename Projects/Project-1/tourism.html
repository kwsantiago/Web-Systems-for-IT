Tourism In Curacao

Kyle W. Santiago | Oct. 3, 2020

Year Total Number of Inbound Tourists
2010 334,645
2011 379,284
2012 405,764
2013 422,151
2014 427,104
2015 408,442
2016 441,356
2017 398,836
2018 431,692

Tourism is one of the biggest industries in Curacao. At least 20% of the economy is contributed to by tourism. In the above table, you can see the amount of inbound toursists to the island. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, tourism has fallen off and the economy has taken a large hit on the island.

Curacao recorded a total of almost 432,000 tourists in 2018, ranking 144th in the world in absolute terms.

By putting the tourist numbers in relation to the population of Curacao the result is put in a better perspective: with 2.7 tourists per resident, Curacao ranks 27th in the world and 9th in Caribbean.
